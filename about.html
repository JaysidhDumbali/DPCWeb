Who We Are

D.P.CHEMICALS is a premier manufacturing unit registered with the Government of Maharashtra as a Small Scale Industry. We specialize in the production of high-quality pharmaceutical excipients that serve as import substitutes, matching distinct international standards such as USP/NF, B.P., E.P., and J.P.

Our manufacturing facility is located at E-10, M.I.D.C. Akkalkot Road, Solapur, where we employ rigorous quality control processes to ensure every batch meets the precise needs of our clients.

Mr. Sidheshwar Dumbali

Chief Managing Director

A qualified expert holding a B.Sc. in Chemistry and B.E. in Mechanical Engineering. His technical expertise guides the company's innovation and commitment to quality.

Recognized Excellence

Our dedication to quality has been recognized by the industry. We are proud recipients of the "Manufacturer of Import Substitute" award, bestowed by the Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Solapur.

This accolade validates our mission to produce world-class excipients locally, adhering to global standards.
